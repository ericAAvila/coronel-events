About Us

Crafted with Culture.
Designed with Love.

At Coronel Events, we believe that every celebration is a reflection of who you are — your roots, your joy, your legacy. From the graceful traditions of a quinceañera to the timeless romance of a wedding day, we pour our hearts into every detail so you can be fully present in every moment.

Serving families across Maryland and South Carolina, we bring elegance, culture, and personalized care to each event we have the honor of planning.

Meet the Planner

Hello, I'm Kenny.

I have always loved creating meaningful experiences that bring people together. From planning campus programs and large-scale events to celebrating life's biggest milestones — weddings and quinces — I have seen firsthand how special a well-planned event can be.

My goal is simple: to help you enjoy your celebration while I handle the details.
